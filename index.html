About

Hello, I'm Leeso.

I'm a tattoo artist based in Seoul, South Korea. I have been working as a tattooist since 2018, and I specialise in colourful and delicate tattoo styles.

As a member of the Korean tattooists' labour union, Tattoo Union, I have completed professional infection control training conducted by Green Hospital, a public-interest medical institution.

I strictly follow Tattoo Union's work and sanitation guidelines to ensure the safety and rights of both clients and tattooists.

I look forward to creating meaningful and beautiful work together. Thank you to everyone who takes interest in my work ✦

Policies

Booking & Deposit

Your appointment is confirmed once a deposit is received. The deposit will be deducted from the final tattoo price. For overseas clients without a Korean bank account, deposits are accepted via PayPal or Wise.

Design & Revisions

The design will be shared at least 3 days before your appointment. You may request up to two rounds of revisions. Additional revisions beyond this may incur an extra fee.

Cancellation & Refund

Cancellations made less than 7 business days before the appointment, or after the design has been completed, are non-refundable. All rights to the reserved design remain with the tattoo artist.

Late Arrival / No-Show

If you arrive more than 60 minutes late without prior notice, the appointment will be considered a no-show and the deposit will be non-refundable.

Appointment Precautions

Please do not schedule other plans on the day of your appointment, as sessions may run longer than expected. After tattooing, please avoid swimming, saunas, intense exercise, and alcohol for a period of time.

Studio Location

The studio is located near Hongdae, Seoul. I will share the detailed location after the design is confirmed.

Booking

Open for bookings.

The studio is located near Hongdae. I do not tattoo minors. My response times can be irregular — please feel free to reach out anytime, and I appreciate your patience if replies are occasionally delayed.

Please describe the tattoo you would like in as much detail as possible. Reference images are highly appreciated.

Placements where ink tends to fade or blur easily, such as fingers, are generally not recommended. Certain difficult placements may require an additional fee. You can finalise the placement on the appointment day after placing the printed stencil on your body.

Please share your preferred dates and time range so I can check my availability.
